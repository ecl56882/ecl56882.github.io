    • Interpersonal Communications Project
      • Interpersonal Communications is a class that teaches students about the meaning of human behavior and human interactions. Before this class, I had no idea our brains, our decision-making process, and our behavioral process were so intricate. I was assigned a group project and my group of 6 had to research and create and present a presentation about the types of romantic love in relationships. From manic love (obsessive lovers) to ludus love (game playing lovers) and all the in betweens, the project was very eye opening. Not only did I learn about the 6 types of romantic love, I also learned how to work with a group from long distance. This assignment was expected to be completed over a holiday break when my group of 6 would not be able to meet up to work. We had to divide and conquer and create a PowerPoint from different states. It was hard to communicate long distance but we ended up with a great presentation and it taught us all how to communicate over distance and still be successful in getting the job done.
